About Invigilator

Welcome to Invigilator, your trusted partner in modern online examination solutions.

At Invigilator, we are committed to revolutionizing the way online exams are conducted. With a focus on accessibility, security, and innovation, we have developed a cutting-edge online examination system that empowers educators and institutions worldwide.

Our platform offers a range of features designed to provide a seamless examination experience for both administrators and test-takers. From AI-powered cheating prevention technology to automated grading and insightful reporting, we're dedicated to making the examination process efficient, secure, and insightful.

With a user-friendly interface, Invigilator ensures that educators can easily create, administer, and manage exams while students can take their exams on any device, anywhere, anytime.

Join us on our journey to transform the future of online assessments. Thank you for choosing Invigilator as your trusted examination partner.
